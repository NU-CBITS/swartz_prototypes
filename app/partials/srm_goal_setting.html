Setting Your Social Rhythm Goals

Setting and meeting SRM goals can help you improve your social rhythms and your mood.

This week you will set one short term SRM goal. The idea is to pick a goal that you think you might be able to accomplish by the end of the week. Don’t pick something too ambitious.

Select one of the following SRM goals--each goal will come with a list of tips and suggestions to help you reach your goal this week.

Pick something that you would like to work on and yet doesn’t seem too overwhelming
