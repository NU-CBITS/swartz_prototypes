The Standard Form of "I" Statements

I feel when I because .

After making an “I” statement, add a positive request for change in the form of a request for a specific action or behavior.

Avoid general requests such as “In the future, I would like you to be nice.”

Make your request as specific as possible, for instance, “In the future, I would like you to pick up more milk when we run out without my having to ask you.”
