Write down a modified schedule for yourself during this event. Think about making as few adjustments as possible. If necessary, get others involved in helping you stay on your schedule to the extent that you can. For instance, if you have house guests, tell them that you keep a strict bed time and ask that everyone keep the noise level down after 10 pm.

After the event is over, what kind of changes to your schedule will you make to get back on track? For instance, when you return from a trip to Europe, how will you change your schedule to re-adjust to your time zone? Could you as a friend to stay with you in the evening so that you don’t fall asleep too early on your first night back at home? Write down your “rebound plan” for getting back on track after the event.
